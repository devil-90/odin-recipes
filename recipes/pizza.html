Vegetarian Margherita Pizza

Description

Indulge in the classic simplicity of our Vegetarian Margherita Pizza. A symphony of flavors featuring a thin, crisp crust, topped with rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. Every bite is a taste of Italy, a perfect balance of textures and freshness. Elevate your pizza night with this timeless favorite that celebrates the essence of authentic and delicious simplicity.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs. olive oil, optional
  • 1 tsp. minced garlic
  • 1 cup basil-flavored tomato or pizza sauce
  • 4 plum tomatoes, cored, seeded and diced
  • 1/4 cup shredded fresh basil plus extra leaves for garnish
  • 2 cups shredded low-fat, regular or soy mozzarella
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F. Prebake fresh pizza crust, if using, for 3 to 4 minutes. Remove from oven, and set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Brush crust with olive oil, if using. Sprinkle with garlic, and spread sauce evenly over top.
  3. Top with diced tomatoes, shredded basil and mozzarella. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup Parmesan.
  4. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, or until cheese melts. Remove from oven, garnish with basil leaves and remaining Parmesan and serve.
